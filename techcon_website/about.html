History

TechCon began in 2015 as a local meetup for developers interested in open-source tools and scalable systems. Over the years it has grown into an international conference with an emphasis on practical engineering, responsible AI, and product design. Each year we add workshops and tracks to reflect the most impactful trends in technology.

Mission

Our mission is simple: empower builders and decision-makers with knowledge, tools, and connections to build technology that benefits everyone. We focus on high-quality talks, hands-on workshops, and safe spaces for networking and mentorship.
